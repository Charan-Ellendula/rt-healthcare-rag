# Employee Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
Company: RT Healthcare
Classification: Confidential

---

## 1. Purpose

This Agreement protects confidential and proprietary information belonging to RT Healthcare.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes:

- Patient information (PHI)
- Business strategies
- Financial data
- Technical documentation
- Source code
- Trade secrets
- Vendor contracts
- Internal policies

---

## 3. Employee Obligations

Employee agrees to:

- Protect Confidential Information
- Use information solely for job duties
- Not disclose to unauthorized parties
- Implement reasonable safeguards
- Return all materials upon termination

---

## 4. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include:

- Publicly available information
- Information required by law to be disclosed
- Information independently developed without company data

---

## 5. Duration

Confidentiality obligations continue:

- During employment
- After termination indefinitely for trade secrets

---

## 6. Remedies

Violation may result in:

- Disciplinary action
- Termination
- Legal action
- Financial damages

---

## 7. Acknowledgment

Employee acknowledges understanding and agrees to comply.
