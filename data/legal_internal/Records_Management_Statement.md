# Records Management Statement
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Compliance
Version: 1.0
Classification: Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes standards for retention, storage, and disposal of organizational records.

---

## 2. Record Categories

- Patient medical records
- Employee records
- Financial records
- Contracts
- Audit logs
- Compliance documentation

---

## 3. Retention Periods

Retention periods follow:

- HIPAA (minimum 6 years)
- IRS requirements
- State healthcare laws
- Contractual obligations

Legal holds override normal retention schedules.

---

## 4. Secure Storage

Records must be stored:

- In encrypted systems
- With restricted access
- In secure physical storage if applicable

---

## 5. Disposal

Records must be disposed of via:

- Secure deletion
- Data wiping
- Certified destruction
- Shredding (for paper)

---

## 6. Legal Hold

If litigation is anticipated:

- Destruction must cease
- Records must be preserved
- Compliance must be notified
