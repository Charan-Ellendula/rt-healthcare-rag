# Internal Intellectual Property (IP) Policy
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Legal & Engineering
Version: 1.0
Classification: Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines ownership and protection of intellectual property developed within RT Healthcare.

---

## 2. Work Product Ownership

All work created:

- During employment
- Using company resources
- Within scope of employment

Is the exclusive property of RT Healthcare.

---

## 3. Covered Intellectual Property

Includes:

- Software code
- Algorithms
- AI models
- Documentation
- Inventions
- Processes
- Designs
- Research findings

---

## 4. Open Source Usage

Employees must:

- Obtain approval before introducing open-source software
- Follow licensing requirements
- Avoid copyleft contamination in proprietary systems

---

## 5. Invention Disclosure

Employees must disclose:

- Patentable inventions
- Novel algorithms
- Unique processes

Company retains filing rights.

---

## 6. Protection Measures

Company may:

- File patents
- Register copyrights
- Enforce trademarks
- Take legal action against infringement

---

## 7. Termination Obligations

Upon termination, employees must:

- Return all IP materials
- Not retain copies
- Maintain confidentiality
