# SOP: Vendor Onboarding
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Compliance & Procurement
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Ensure vendors meet security and compliance standards before engagement.

---

# 2. Due Diligence Steps

1. Risk classification
2. Security questionnaire
3. Review compliance certifications
4. BAA execution (if PHI involved)
5. Contract review
6. Access approval

---

# 3. System Access

- Vendor-specific account
- MFA mandatory
- Least privilege
- Activity logging

---

# 4. Ongoing Monitoring

- Annual reassessment
- Compliance certification review
- Performance review
