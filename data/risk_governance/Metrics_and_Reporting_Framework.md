# Metrics and Reporting Framework
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Security & Compliance
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines performance metrics to measure effectiveness of security and compliance programs.

---

# 2. Security Metrics

- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)
- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR)
- Patch compliance rate
- Open critical vulnerabilities
- Phishing simulation failure rate

---

# 3. Compliance Metrics

- Policy review completion rate
- Training completion rate
- Audit finding closure rate
- Vendor reassessment completion rate

---

# 4. Reporting Frequency

- Monthly operational dashboard
- Quarterly executive summary
- Annual board report
