Owner: Risk Management
Version: 2.0

# 1. Purpose

Central repository for documenting enterprise risks.

---

# 2. Required Fields

- Risk ID
- Risk Description
- Risk Category
- Asset Affected
- Threat Source
- Vulnerability
- Inherent Likelihood
- Inherent Impact
- Inherent Risk Score
- Existing Controls
- Residual Risk Score
- Risk Owner
- Mitigation Plan
- Target Completion Date
- Status
- Review Date
