# Operational KPIs
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Operations & Security
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines key performance indicators to measure operational effectiveness and system reliability.

---

# 2. Availability Metrics

- System uptime percentage
- SLA compliance rate
- Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)

---

# 3. Incident Metrics

- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)
- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR)
- Incident recurrence rate

---

# 4. Security Metrics

- Patch compliance rate
- Open critical vulnerabilities
- MFA adoption rate
- Phishing failure rate

---

# 5. Compliance Metrics

- Policy review completion rate
- Audit finding closure rate
- Vendor reassessment completion rate

---

# 6. Reporting

KPIs reported:

- Monthly to management
- Quarterly to executive leadership
- Annually to board
