# Escalation Matrix
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Operations
Version: 1.0
Classification: Internal

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines structured escalation procedures for operational and security incidents.

---

# 2. Incident Severity Levels

Severity 1 – Critical (Service outage, data breach)
Severity 2 – High (Major functionality impaired)
Severity 3 – Medium (Limited impact)
Severity 4 – Low (Minor issue)

---

# 3. Escalation Timeline

Severity 1:
- Immediate notification
- Executive escalation within 30 minutes

Severity 2:
- Escalation within 1 hour

Severity 3:
- Escalation within 4 hours

Severity 4:
- Standard ticket workflow

---

# 4. Escalation Roles

Level 1 – On-call engineer  
Level 2 – Engineering manager  
Level 3 – Director/VP  
Level 4 – Executive leadership  

---

# 5. Communication Channels

- Incident management system
- Secure messaging
- Email notification
- Status page updates
