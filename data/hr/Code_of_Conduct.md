# Code of Conduct  
**Company:** RT Healthcare  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose
This Code of Conduct outlines ethical standards and behavioral expectations for all employees and representatives of RT Healthcare.

## 2. Core Principles
Employees must:
- Act with integrity
- Maintain professionalism
- Protect confidential information
- Comply with all laws and regulations
- Promote patient safety and quality care

## 3. Ethical Standards
Employees shall:
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Not accept inappropriate gifts or bribes
- Provide accurate documentation
- Maintain honest communications

## 4. Confidentiality & HIPAA
Employees must protect:
- Patient health information (PHI)
- Employee personal data
- Proprietary business information

Unauthorized disclosure may result in termination and legal penalties.

## 5. Workplace Conduct
Employees must:
- Treat colleagues respectfully
- Avoid discrimination or harassment
- Maintain professional communication
- Follow safety protocols

## 6. Use of Company Resources
Company assets must be used for legitimate business purposes only.

## 7. Reporting Violations
Suspected violations must be reported to management or HR.

## 8. Enforcement
Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.
