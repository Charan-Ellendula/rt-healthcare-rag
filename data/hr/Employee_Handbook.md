# Employee Handbook  
**Company:** RT Healthcare  
**Version:** 1.0  

## 1. Welcome
Welcome to RT Healthcare. We are committed to excellence in patient care, ethical operations, and employee growth.

## 2. Employment Relationship
Employment is at-will unless otherwise specified in writing.

## 3. Equal Employment Opportunity
RT Healthcare provides equal employment opportunities without discrimination.

## 4. Work Hours
Standard workweek: 40 hours (unless otherwise specified).
Overtime is governed by federal and state law.

## 5. Compensation
Employees are paid according to established payroll schedules.
Deductions comply with legal requirements.

## 6. Benefits
Benefits may include:
- Health insurance
- Dental and vision coverage
- Retirement plans
- Paid time off
- Professional development support

## 7. Workplace Policies
Employees must follow:
- Anti-Harassment Policy
- Code of Conduct
- Safety Policy
- HIPAA Compliance Policy

## 8. IT & Security
Employees must:
- Use secure passwords
- Protect company systems
- Follow cybersecurity protocols

## 9. Disciplinary Process
RT Healthcare may apply progressive discipline where appropriate.

## 10. Acknowledgment
Employees must sign acknowledgment of receipt of this handbook.
