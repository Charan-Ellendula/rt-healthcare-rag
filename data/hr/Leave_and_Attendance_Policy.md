# Leave and Attendance Policy  
**Company:** RT Healthcare  
**Version:** 1.0  

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes attendance expectations and leave entitlements.

## 2. Attendance Expectations
Employees must:
- Report to work on time
- Notify supervisors of absences in advance where possible

Excessive absenteeism may result in disciplinary action.

## 3. Types of Leave

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
Accrued based on length of service.

### Sick Leave
For personal illness or medical appointments.

### Family and Medical Leave (FMLA)
Provided in accordance with federal law.

### Bereavement Leave
Up to 3 days for immediate family (or as defined by policy).

### Jury Duty
Employees will be granted leave as required by law.

### Military Leave
Complies with USERRA regulations.

## 4. Unapproved Absences
Failure to report absence for 3 consecutive days may be considered job abandonment.

## 5. Remote Work Attendance
Remote employees must remain available during agreed working hours.
