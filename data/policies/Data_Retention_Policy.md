# Data Retention Policy
Company: RT Healthcare
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines retention and disposal requirements for organizational data.

---

# 2. Retention Periods

Medical records: per HIPAA and state law  
Financial records: 7 years  
Audit logs: 7 years  
Security logs: 1 year  

---

# 3. Secure Disposal

- Secure deletion
- Data wiping
- Certified destruction
- Shredding for paper records
